    For those interested. The word 'curry' occurs in Dasam Granth:

     

    ਸਤੂਅਨ ਕਰੀ ਬਨਾਇ ਕੈ ਤਾ ਮੈ ਬਧ੍ਯੋ ਬਨਾਇ ॥

    After making the curry of barley she placed in it a statue carved out flour dough.
