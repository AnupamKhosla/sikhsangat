      On 5/11/2016 at 1:57 PM, jkvlondon said:

    The singhs could only see the round tummy and the basically nanga sareer , the simple club in his hand and said what can he do ?

    I'm sorry but Gurbani does not merely recommend relying on mantra (even if it's the Gurmantra).

    When Babur invaded India, religious figures attempted to use mantras to stop the invasion and failed:

    ਕੋਟੀ ਹੂ ਪੀਰ ਵਰਜਿ ਰਹਾਏ ਜਾ ਮੀਰੁ ਸੁਣਿਆ ਧਾਇਆ ॥
    When they heard of invasion of emperor Babar, then millions of religious leaders failed to halt him.

    ਥਾਨ ਮੁਕਾਮ ਜਲੇ ਬਿਜ ਮੰਦਰ ਮੁਛਿ ਮੁਛਿ ਕੁਇਰ ਰੁਲਾਇਆ ॥ He burned houses, resting places and strong palaces and the princes, cut into pieces, he causes to roll in dust.

    ਕੋਈ ਮੁਗਲੁ ਨ ਹੋਆ ਅੰਧਾ ਕਿਨੈ ਨ ਪਰਚਾ ਲਾਇਆ ॥੪॥
    No Mughal became blind and no one wrought any miracle. p417

    Read the whole shabad for Guru Nanak Dev ji's description of the invasion.

     

    I fully realize and accept that God can do anything whenever he wants in an instant:

    ਕੀੜਾ ਥਾਪਿ ਦੇਇ ਪਾਤਿਸਾਹੀ ਲਸਕਰ ਕਰੇ ਸੁਆਹ ॥
    A worm, He appoints to kingship and He reduces an army to ashes. p144

     

    But that doesn't mean we should depend on in the normal case. In the normal case, we have to make worldly provisions.

    Sukhmani Sahib says God can keep someone alive without breath and I fully accept this:

    ਪ੍ਰਭ ਭਾਵੈ ਬਿਨੁ ਸਾਸ ਤੇ ਰਾਖੈ ॥
    prabh bhāvai bin sās tē rākhai .
    If it pleases the Lord He preserves the body without breath

    But that does not mean we're supposed to hold our breath.

    God can sustain someone without food. So why do we have langar?
