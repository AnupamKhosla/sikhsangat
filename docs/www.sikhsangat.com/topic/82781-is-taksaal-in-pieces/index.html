    Exactly.

    Something everyone needs to take note of

     within this past week taksal has accomplished 2 major things for the panth. 

    1. Completion of janam asthan gurduara for sant baba jarnail singh ji khalsa

    2. Completion of Thara Sahib gurduara within darbar sahib.

    Somethings that should be noted about Damdami Taksal-

    1. It was started by 10th guru, guru gobind singh ji maharaj

    2. Each mukhi has always been one of the greatest mahapursh's of their time and have done very important things for the panth.

     

    Taksal is not just some organization or committee, there is nothing really like it in the world, because this is a group that guru sahib started themselves. People need to reconsider their thoughts and words when they say Taksal is falling or has been driven in the dirt and etc. When they say these sort of things, they are insulting all of Taksal, including all previous mukhi's, all singh and singhnia who were in Taksal and all of their jeevans and their sacrifices, and even Guru Sahib themselves. Also people should really reconsider their thoughts and words when they talk about the state of the panth, and the state of sikhi. We should never say Sikhi is dying, or people are killing it, it will never happen. People from all over the world have tried, the greatest empires in the world, and all have failed. Sikhi isn't something that will be wiped off the Earth. We need to remember that Guru sahib is always watching over us as a panth and as individuals, because we are their children. The chaar sahibzade are always standing by as are all shaheed singh's, and all sikhs in sachkhand. Don't worry about the state of Taksal or the state  of the panth. Worry about the state of your own sikhi.The rest will fall into place.
