The future population of Africa is 2.5 Billion by the year 2050 and projected to be over 4 Billion plus by the year 2100.

    Punjabi's have had the truth of Sikhi before them for 500years and yet only 10% of the historical Punjab population (Pakistani Punjab, east Punjab, Haryana and Himachal) is Sikh today.     Of course before all that, we have to ensure that Muslims, Christians, Derawadi's (Hindutva proxies) and Manuwadi's (Hindutva castesists) do not jointly leave Sikhs as a minority in east Punjab by the 2031 census.

    Sikhs were 5% of the Punjabi population around the year 1800 and we were a minority in all districts of Punjab in 1947 when our numbers had risen to 13% of all Punjabi's, so we are used to being a minority.

    But now it is time for Ekta within the Panth and for us to drop labels and hypenated identities and ensure only one united Gurdwara per pind and locality in the West and educate outsiders as to why Sikhi is the solution to all their problems (and globally) and why they should also be part of the Sikh Panth. When Sikhs get to 100million strong that will be when we can truly do Sarbat da Bhala.
