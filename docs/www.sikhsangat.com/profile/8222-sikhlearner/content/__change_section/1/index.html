    3. Shortened Vs full rehraas sahib

      SikhLearner replied to Damdamitaksaal84's topic in WHAT'S HAPPENING?

      As Gursikhs - please stop starting a sentence with 'i am...'. A Gursikh is 'not'. We do not exist. As a gursikh our aim is to get rid of 'i'. Anyone you like or dislike for that sake is vaheguru. Please remember there is no you or i, its all him. Any bani is bani, the more bani we recite (with out mind focus on vaheguru) is amazing
